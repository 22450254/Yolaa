    Our Story

    Lucky Foodie is a website for ording delicous foods from various areas online. It was established by a MA student of COMM named Yola in 2023. She believes that the food in Hong Kong is not too good, so she set up this website to satisfy herself and others. There are mainly four kinds of cuisine: fast-food from America, Janpanese snacks, Korean food and Sichuan cuisine. You could choose your target food by the link and then wait for it in the reality.
